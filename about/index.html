About

My name is Minh-Tâm. For the 2024–25 academic year, I am a Gibbs Assistant Professor in the mathematics department at Yale. I’m also co-organizing the Yale Geometry, Physics, and Symmetry Seminar.

From 2020 through 2024, I was a CLE Moore Instructor at MIT. From 2014 through 2020, I was a graduate student at UChicago, advised by Châu Ngô and Victor Ginzburg.
