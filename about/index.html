About

My name is Minh-Tâm. Starting in the Fall 2025 semester, I will be a tenure-track assistant professor in the mathematics department at Howard University.

For the 2024–25 academic year, I was a Gibbs Assistant Professor in the mathematics department at Yale, co-organizing the Geometry, Physics, and Symmetry Seminar. From 2020 through 2024, I was a CLE Moore Instructor at MIT. From 2014 through 2020, I was a graduate student at UChicago, advised by Châu Ngô and Victor Ginzburg.
