3. Scripts

txt Permutations taking the ordering of simple reflections in Sage to those in GAP3

Scripts to pre-process CHEVIE data:
join_lines.py* Joins broken lines in CHEVIE output
csv_families.py* Converts truncated Lusztig families to CSV
csv_icc.py* Converts \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) to CSV, then evaluates at \(x = 1\), then inverts
extract_icc_labels.py* Extracts \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) ordering of irreducible characters and local systems
find_permutations.py* Finds permutation taking \(\mathtt{ChevieCharInfo}\) ordering to \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) ordering
Scripts to compute the \(\alpha_z^\psi\) and \(\alpha_z^\mathcal{F}\), and test conjectures:
permute_vectors.py* Permutes transformed character values into \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) ordering
solve_vectors.py* Solves for \(\alpha_z^\psi(\mathsf{v})\) by multiplying vectors by inverse matrices of ungraded Springer multiplicities
iterate_sortable.sage Iterates over Coxeter elements \(c\), printing out the \(c\)-sortable elements
test_solved.py* Tests nonzero coefficients of \(\alpha_z^\psi\) for unimodal absolute values, mixed signs, or strictly negative signs; searches exceptions for \(c\)-sortable elements, for all \(c\)
sum_families.py* Sums \(\alpha_z^\psi\) for \(\psi \in \mathcal{F}\) to obtain \(\alpha_z^\mathcal{F}\)
test_summed.py Tests nonzero coefficients of \(\alpha_z^\mathcal{F}\) for unimodal absolute values, mixed signs, or strictly negative signs; searches exceptions for \(c\)-sortable elements, for all \(c\)
