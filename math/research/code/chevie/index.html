3. Scripts

Python scripts written mostly by Claude.*

Pre-processing CHEVIE data:

join_lines.py Joins broken lines in CHEVIE output
csv_families.py Converts truncated Lusztig families to CSV
csv_icc.py Converts \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) to CSV, then evaluates at \(x = 1\), then inverts
extract_icc_labels.py Extracts \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) ordering of irreducible characters
find_permutations.py Finds permutation taking \(\mathtt{ChevieCharInfo}\) ordering to \(\mathtt{ICCTable}\) ordering

Carrying out calculations and testing the conjecture:

sort_vectors.py 1. Sorts transformed character values according to permutation
trim_vectors.py 2. Trims away duplicate vectors of transformed Hecke character values
solve_vectors.py 3. Solves for \(\alpha_z^\psi(\mathsf{v})\) by multiplying vectors by inverse matrices of ungraded Springer multiplicities
sum_families.py 4. Sums up \(\alpha_z^\psi(\mathsf{v})\) over \(\psi \in \mathcal{F}\) to get \(\alpha_z^\mathcal{F}(\mathsf{v})\)
test_conjecture.py 5. Tests whether nonzero coefficients of \(\alpha_z^\mathcal{F}(\mathsf{v})\) have unimodal absolute values, mixed signs, or strictly negative signs
